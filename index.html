FAQ
§ 08 — QUESTIONS

Deep Dive into HOOD USDP

Q.01

How do I send USDG to a phone number?

+

You enter a phone number and an amount, and pick a privacy tier. Your USDG locks in the Hood USDP SMS escrow contract on Robinhood Chain, and the recipient gets an SMS with a secure, one-time claim link. When they tap it, a wallet is created for them on the fly and the USDG is released. If they never claim, the funds auto-refund to you after a timeout — no lost money, no dead-ends.

Q.02

How is privacy actually enforced on-chain?

+

Balances are stored as FHE ciphertexts, so contracts can add, subtract, and compare amounts without ever seeing plaintext. Transfers additionally pass through a ZK shielded pool, so the link between sender and recipient can't be reconstructed from on-chain data.

Q.03

What are the three privacy tiers?

+

Full Private hides amount, sender, and recipient. Semi-Private hides the amount only. Standard is fully public with the lowest fee — you choose the trade-off on every payment.

Q.04

Why Robinhood Chain and USDG?

+

Robinhood Chain is an EVM L2 on Arbitrum Orbit purpose-built for regulated finance, and USDG is a reserve-backed, regulated stablecoin issued by Paxos. Together they give Hood USDP fast, low-cost settlement without giving up regulatory footing.

Q.05

Do recipients need ETH or a wallet to receive money?

+

No. A wallet is created for the recipient automatically when they tap their claim link, and gas is sponsored, so they never need to hold ETH or manage a seed phrase just to receive a payment.

Q.06

What does HUSDP actually do?

+

HUSDP is the protocol token. Holders can stake it to run relay nodes, provide shielded-pool liquidity, or simply stake for a share of protocol fees and USDG reserve yield.
